BP Recorder - How to open the app
=================================

You do NOT need GitHub.

1. Download or save the file named:
   BP-Recorder.html

2. Put it somewhere easy to find, such as:
   - Desktop
   - Downloads

3. Double-click BP-Recorder.html

4. It should open in your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox).

That is the app.

Tips
----
- Keep the file. Your readings are saved in the browser on this computer.
- You need an internet connection the first time so the chart library can load.
- To back up your readings, use the Export CSV button inside the app.
- Do NOT type https://index.html into the browser. Just double-click the file.

If double-click does not open it:
- Right-click BP-Recorder.html
- Choose Open With
- Select Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox
