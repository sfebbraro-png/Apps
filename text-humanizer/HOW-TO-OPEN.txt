Text Humanizer - How to open
============================

1. Download Text-Humanizer.html
2. Save it to Desktop or Downloads
3. Double-click the file
4. Paste text on the left
5. Review findings and fixes on the right

Direct raw download link after publish:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sfebbraro-png/Apps/main/Text-Humanizer.html

If the page shows "429 Too Many Requests", wait a minute and try the raw link again,
or download the repo zip and open Text-Humanizer.html from the unzipped folder.
